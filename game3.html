Game 3: Blackjack

Welcome to Blackjack!

How to play:

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer.

You will recieve two cards face up, and the dealer will recieve two cards, with one face down. If you don't think your hand will beat the dealer's, click "hit" to draw another card. If you do think your hand will beat the dealer's, click "stand". Once you do, the dealer will reveal the hidden card, and draw until their hand is worth at least 17. If the dealer's hand goes over 21 or your hand is worth more, you win 50 chips! However, if you go over 21 first, the dealer wins. If your hand is worth exactly 21 and you only have 2 cards in your hand, you win 75 chips.

Number cards are worth the value on the card. Face cards (King, Queen, Jack) are worth 10. Aces are worth either 1 or 11, whichever is more helpful.
