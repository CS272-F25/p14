Space Dodge

Survive as long as you can by dodging enemy ships in space.

Controls: Use arrow keys to move your ship left, right, up, or down.

Objective: Avoid enemies, stay alive, and get the highest score you can.

Tips: Keep moving, stay near the center, and predict enemy rebounds.

Cave of Mysteries

Answer riddles to reach the hidden treasure!

Controls: Simply type your answer for each onscreen riddle into the box and hit submit!

Objective: Answer the onscreen riddle in exact words to progress through the stages and make it to the treasure.

Tips: Each riddle comes with a scrambled version of the answer to point you in the right direction. If you're still stuck, you can always skip.

Game 3

Challenge the dealer and score as many chips as you can in this classic card game.

Controls: Click "hit" to draw another card, or click "stand" to compare your hand to the dealer's.

Objective: Score higher than the dealer without going over 21.

Tips: Kings, Queens, and Jacks are worth 10, while Aces can be worth 1 or 11. The dealer will always draw until the value of their hand is 17 or higher.
